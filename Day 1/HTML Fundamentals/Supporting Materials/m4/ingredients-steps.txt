
        2 T Wired Brain Coffee Hot Cocoa Mix
        8 oz Milk
        Marshmallows (optional)
      



 	 Scoop Hot Cocoa Mix into a mug.
         Heat milk on the stove or in the microwave to just below a boil.
 	 Pour milk into a mug, stirring gently as you pour.
	 Stir until mix is fully dissolved in the milk.
	 Top it with marshmallows.</li>

  Cocoa is the powdered substance left after processing cacao beans and
  has little or no cacao butter.

  Chocolate is basically cocoa with the cacao butter and often sugar and milk.
